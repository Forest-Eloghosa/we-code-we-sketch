Our Story

Founded by Forest Eloghosa, We Code We Sketch was born from a love of creativity and a passion for personal storytelling. Whether through custom websites or hand-drawn portraits, we create work that reflects each client’s unique voice. Built on transparency, heart, and hands-on craftsmanship, we’re here to turn your ideas into experiences.

Whether you're a small business, an individual looking for a personal site, or someone who wants a unique piece of artwork — you’re in the right place.

We bring together the structure of front-end development and the freedom of sketch artistry to tell your story — the way it deserves to be told.

Frequently Asked Questions

Have a question about working with We Code We Sketch? We’ve answered a few common ones below.

Each project begins with a personal consultation to understand your goals, audience, and vision. Whether it's a business website or a personal gift, I tailor both code and artwork to match your unique story.

Typically, we can begin within 1–3 business days after your initial inquiry and agreement on scope. For urgent requests, I offer flexible scheduling where possible.

Pricing varies based on the project’s size and complexity. I provide transparent quotes, and depending on the scope, a small upfront deposit may be required. There are no hidden fees or long-term contracts unless requested.

I offer flexible hourly or project-based arrangements, depending on your needs. Weekly availability ranges from 5–20 hours. Larger projects can be scheduled in advance.

I specialize in front-end web development with HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, responsive design, accessibility, and Lightbox integrations — combined with custom digital and traditional sketch artistry.

Not at all — I welcome both business and personal commissions. Whether it's a website for your startup, a one-of-a-kind gift for a loved one or an amazing piece of art for yourself , I’ve got you covered.
